Voice Stress Recognition Software

Project Summary

Our project is a machine learning program that determines the amount of stress a person has from voice recordings. Our group is taking 200 voice samples per person for a total of 600 voice samples. Each time we take a voice sample, we record the date, time, stress (yes or no), and the amount of sleep we had. There are other attributes we record (such as if we drank, if we are sick, if we smoked, if we are on medicine, our blood pressure, heart rate, and body temperature). We process these samples, and the computer will turn the voice samples into a variety of numbers corresponding to different parameters. We will train a machine to recognize when our teammates are stressed, and try to implement a machine learning algorithm that can categorize whether a person is stressed or unstressed from the numbers generated from the voice recognition software. Our goal at the end of the project is to have a predictive algorithm that can tell if a person is stressed or unstressed from a voice sample.

Our Voice Sampling Script

Do each 3 times:
Hold "a" (ah) for 10 seconds.
Hold "i" (ee) for 10 seconds.
"Pea," "Tea," "Key" 10 times.

"When the sunlight strikes raindrops in the air, they act as a prism and form a rainbow. The rainbow is a division of white light into many beautiful colors. These take the shape of a long round arch, with its path high above, and its two ends apparently beyond the horizon."

Then, speak spontaneously for 15 seconds.
